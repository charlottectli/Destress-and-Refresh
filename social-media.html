Social Media Stress

Teenagers spend so much time on social media every day. The resources below will teach you how to avoid the pressure and stress, such as comparing yourself to others, that comes with it.

Positive Affirmations Exercise


Positive affirmations are positive phrases that can change the way you think about yourself. Rpeating the statements below aloud multiple times can help build a more optimistic mindset that could allieve your stress. Feel free to add affirimations of your own that are suited for your own circumstances!

1. I love and accept myself just as I am.
2. I am loved and supported by the ones around me.
3. I already have everything I need.
4. I'm in control of my own happiness.
5. I'm as worthy as everyone else.
6. I'm deserving of happiness and success.
7. I'm grateful for my many blessings.
8. I'm perfect the way I am no matter what others say.
9. I'm in control of the people and things I surround myself with.
10. I'm enough.
