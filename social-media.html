Social Media Stress

Teenagers spend so much time on social media every day. The resources below will teach you how to avoid the pressure and stress that comes with it.





☆ Social Pressure on Social Media

This short video discusses how we shouldn't let social media define who we are. Many of us strive to build a community through interacting with posts and receiving likes, but we must realize that connecting with ourselves first is more important.




☆ How to Avoid Social Media Stress

This article from Mellowmed gives us some tips on how a person can avoid social media stress, such as managing our time on the platforms and asessing the way we use them.




☆ Young Minds - Social Media and Mental Health

Young Minds reminds how we can spend our time online positively, as well as how to deal with online bullying. There's also extra articles at the bottom that are also related to social media stress that you may find relevant to you.
