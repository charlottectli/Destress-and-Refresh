Anxiety & Depression

Dealing with anxiety and depression can be extremely difficult, especially on top of everything else a teenager faces. It's important to take care of yourself mentally - below are some resources that may help.





☆ The 5-4-3-2-1 Method

The 5-4-3-2-1 Method is a grounding exercise designed to calm you that can be done anywhere. It helps you stay in the moment and avoid negative thoughts by paying attention to your surroundings using your 5 senses.



☆ Instagram @DLC Anxiety

The vision of DLC Anxiety is to connect like minded people so they can support each other through their anxiety experiences. DLC Anxiety's Instagram page posts a variety of content ranging from artwork, videos, and infographics to spread awarness about the struggles people face and how to combat them.




☆ Instagram @Crazy Head Comics

Crazy Head Comics is run by Swedish artist Matilda Heindow. Her Instagram page posts useful information about mental health, for example what different kinds of mental disorders look like, how to engage in self care, and more. Her colorful designs and thoughtful words might just be the support you need!
