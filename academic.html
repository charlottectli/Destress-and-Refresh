Academic Stress

Academic stress is nearly inevitable because school can definitely be hard at times! But with the right mindset and work habits, you'll be able to get through it.

Journaling Activity

This is a journaling activity that aims to relieve academic stress by using the Cognitive Behavioral Therapy's ABC Approach (antecedents, behaviors, consequences). All data collected from this form is anonymous and will be saved on your laptop.

Young Minds' Problems at School page describes academically related problems that may be relevant to your life. At the bottom, there are links to other pages on the website that talks about similar topics as well.
