Academic Stress

Academic stress is nearly inevitable because school can definitely be hard at times! But with the right mindset and work habits, you'll be able to get through it. Here are some resources that can help:






☆ Stressed Out Students Infographic

This infographic shows statistics about stressed students - 1 in 5 students are academically stressed, causing them to want to drop out of school and not hanging out with friends. It also provides various ways you can deal with your stress, such as finding a strong support system and staying active.




☆ Young Minds - Problems at School

Young Minds' Problems at School page describes academically related problems that may be relevant to your life. As you scroll down, you'll see inspirational quotes and what you can do to get through those stressful times. At the bottom, there are links to other pages on the website that talks about similar topics as well.




☆ NHS - Student Stress

This website specifically talks about student stress. It provides tips and ideas on how you can deal with your stress as well as when you should seek help. A general practioner also discusses stress in a short video at the very end.
