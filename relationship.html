Relationship Stress

Everyone has conflict with their family, friends, or partner from time to time. If you want to know how you can resolve the tension respectfully and effectively, take a look at the images and links provided.

Resources






☆ Tips On Conflict Management

This image gives you tips on how you can resolve conflict with those around you. It reminds us that we can talk it out with the other person in a respectful manner while also taking care of yourself during this stressful time.




☆ Better Health Channel - Family Conflict

Better Health Channel reassures us that it's normal for family members to disagree occasionally, and provides us with multiple tips to work it out. If the situation is serious and you need professional help, there's a section at the bottom of the website that may be helpful.




☆ Young Minds - Friends

Young Minds discusses many kinds of friendship conflicts, such as peer pressure and toxic friends. A unique thing about this website is that there's advice from young people like you who have gone through similar situations, making their help more genuine and relevant to you!
